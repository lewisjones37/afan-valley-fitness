Our Story

Afan Valley Fitness is a community-run gym located in the heart of Cymmer in the South Wales valleys. It was founded in 2019 by a group of fitness enthusiasts who were tired of having to travel long distances to access quality gym facilities. The founders pooled their resources and set up camp in the old unused leisure center, which they then converted into a state-of-the-art fitness center.

The gym began to gain a reputation as a friendly and welcoming place where members could exercise in a supportive and encouraging environment. The gym's success was built on its commitment to providing high-quality equipment, knowledgeable trainers, and a range of fitness classes to suit all abilities.

Following COIVD, the gym underwent a major renovation to accommodate its growing membership. A new cardio section, weight section, and core strength area were added, along with a dedicated deadlift room for powerlifters. The gym also has an outdoor astroturf area, where teams regularly host training sessions. It also allowed members to work out in the fresh air during the summer months.

Today, Afan Valley Fitness is one of the most popular gyms in the area, with a loyal membership base that continues to grow. The gym's success is a testament to the power of community, and its founders' vision of creating a place where people can come together to achieve their fitness goals.
